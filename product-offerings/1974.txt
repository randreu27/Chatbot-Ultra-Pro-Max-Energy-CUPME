Introduction  
Siemens Canada Limited (SCL) enters into an agreement with a supplier for the purchase and supply of 
Deliverables on the basis that such Deliverables will be fully contract compliant.  
 
Without prejudice to SCL other rights and remedies (whether in contrac t or at law), for every occurrence of  
quality  issues listed below, SCL  will request from the supplier the payment of the amounts stated below 
representing an administrative charge to mitigate damages occurred in relation with the non -quality event. 
Without any other notice, SCL may deduct the amount  from any payment due to the Supplier.  
 
Description  
The internal administration costs or damages incurred by SCL include, non - limitatively, the associated  
processing and technical assessment undertaken by SCL due to the occurrence of the following quali ty  
issues under the Suppliers’ responsibility:  
 
 Rejects at receipt  
 Concessions  
 Rejects as a result of complaints (Siemens, end customer, operator), etc  
 
Global standard charges (US dollars)  
Concessions  submissions (New, Revised and Rejects) : $600 
Rejects at receipt:  $850 
Rejects as a result of assembly/test complaints:  $1100  
Rejects as a result of customer/operator complaints:  $1800 
  
 
 
 
 
The revised administrative charges noted above are a good faith estimate of the internal administration costs and 
damages suffered by SCL and are not penalties associated with the relevant quality incidents.  These will be 
applied starting 1st January 2016.
