Introduction . This Notice to Suppliers (NTS) covers the latest to the Energy Business Quality 
Requirements (E BQR) for all Siemens Energy (SE) for Aeroderivative Gas Turbine (AGT) 
Suppliers, Maintenance Repair Overhaul Centers, and Repair Vendors (Suppliers).  
 
Scope/Applicability.  The purpose of the EBQR is to formally communicate the SE Supplier 
Quality guidelines and expectations to the global AGT supply chain. In this latest version of 
the EBQR some minor changes have been done to integrate and harmonize  the requirements . 
 
Main changes:   Material compliance requirements ( Section 8.1), ND A requirements (Section 
8.4.3), Shelf -life requirements  (Section 8.5.4) and NCC currency (Section 10)

within the AGT section. If you require further information, please contact your Supplier 
Quality representative.
