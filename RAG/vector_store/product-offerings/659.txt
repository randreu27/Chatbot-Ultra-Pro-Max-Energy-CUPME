Centrifugal / axial compressors control functionality Our control solutions for centrifugal and axial compressors feature patented anti-surge and load sharing algorithms, ensuring optimal performance and protection. Key functionalities include: Antisurge control: Accurate and auto compensation for shifts in surge line under varying gas conditions (MWt, T, Z) to ensure maximum operating range without recycling gas. Performance control: Advanced algorithms to maintain optimal compressor performance across varying operating conditions. Pressure and flow control/limiting: Precise control mechanisms to regulate pressure and flow, ensuring stable and efficient operation. Load sharing and master/slave optimization: Intelligent load sharing capabilities to balance the load between multiple compressors, optimizing overall system performance. Normal start/stop sequencing logic: Sophisticated logic to ensure smooth and reliable automated start/stop sequences, minimizing wear and tear on equipment. Emergency shutdown and protection logic: Robust safety mechanisms to protect your compressors in case of emergencies, ensuring safe and reliable operation. Process reciprocating compressors control functionality Our control solutions for process reciprocating compressors are designed to meet API 618 compliance standards, offering a range of functionalities to enhance performance and reliability: Capacity control and/or Infinite Step Control: Advanced capacity control mechanisms to ensure precise regulation of compressor output, optimizing efficiency and performance. Normal start/stop package sequencing logic: Reliable sequencing logic to ensure smooth and controlled start/stop operations, reducing mechanical stress and extending equipment life. Frame and running gear monitoring: Continuous monitoring of the compressor frame and running gear to detect and address potential issues before they lead to failures. Lube oil system control and cylinder lubrication monitoring: Comprehensive control and monitoring of the lubrication system and compressor cylinders to ensure optimal performance and prevent damage. Coolant system monitoring: Continuous monitoring of the coolant system to ensure proper cooling and prevent overheating, ensuring reliable compressor operation.
