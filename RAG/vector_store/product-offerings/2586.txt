1 PURPOSE  & SCOPE  
The purpose of this procedure is to detail the process for the use of the Dry Mate Plug Connector Assembly 
Fixture s, TC6A -658 and TC6A -1387 .  
 
The TC6A -658 assembly fixture is used during the on -site installation of the Dry Mate Plug Connector DHCE -
A70…  series of conne ctors. The TC6A -1387 is almost identical to the TC6A -658, however it has an insert for 
testing the DHCE -A51…  series of conne ctors. The relevant assembl y fixture is fitted during oil testing of the Dry 
Mate Plug if the mating Tubing Hanger Dry Mate Receptacle is not available during the testing.  
 
The Maximum Allowable Working Pressure (MAWP) of the Assembly Fixture is 16,500 psi (1,137,6 bar) . THIS 
MUST  NOT BE EXCEEDED.  
 
 
The Test Adaptor is suitable for repeat testing. As long as the prerequisite checks shown in Section 5 are 
satisfied.

• For full Safety and Hazard information, please consult the ElecTRON IOM or Safety Data Sheet for the

Caution shall be exercised during assembly to ensure that fittings and hydraulic/pneumatic

5 PREPARATION  
• Visually inspect the assembly, ensure there is no damage to the sealing cone,  elastomeric seal,  threads, 
or body of the Assembly Fixture. If there is any doubt over the integrity of the Assembly Fixture  or any 
of its components , stop work immediately , quarantine the equipment  and contact the Siemens Energy 
Technical Department . 
• Clean Assembly Fixture with isopropyl alcohol impregnated wipes, paying particular attention to the 
sealing cone face.   
6 PROCESS  
1. Clamp the extended flats of Assembly Fixture in a vice with soft jaw grips.  
2. Fit Dry Mate Plug body into Assembly Fixture and tighten connector Clamp Ring (Item 20 on 
Connector Drawings ). 
3. Torque clamp ring to 40 lbf∙ft  (54 Nm).  
4. Carry out required Pressure Testing as stipulated in relevant Installation Procedure  and Pressure 
Fitting Operating Instructions. These are referenced in the applicable connector Product Data Sheets.  
5. On completion of testing,  reduce pressure to ambient,  remove Assembly Fixture .  
6. Repeat visual inspection  of Assembly Fixture sealing surface and O -Seal. 
7. Clean up any residual oil on connector and Assembly Fixture.  
 
END OF PROCEDURE
